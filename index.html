SUBSYSTEMS

SATELLITE DIVISION

  • Attitude Determination And Control System (ADCS)

    It deals with determining and controlling the satellite's attitude as per the requirements. It is also very essential for stabilising the satellite in orbit and ensuring that it points in the direction it is supposed to point in.

  • Electrical Power System (EPS)

    Dealing with generating, distributing and regulating the power supply for the entire satellite.

  • Ground Control & Communication (G&C)

    The Communications subsystem is the subsystem which lets us know if our satellite is alive in orbit. If Communications fails, the satellite is a good rock in space. It is responsible for ensuring telecommunication between the satellite and the ground station.

  • Onboard Data Handling System (ODHS)

    Programs the brain of the satellite to perform its desired functions and operate all components on- board, including storing and forwarding data.

  • PAYLOAD

    The Payload System Involves the selection, integration and maintenance of the instruments required to perform the desired functionality (payload idea) of the satellite.

  • SATELLITE DIVISION Structures and Thermal (S & T)

    The thermal and structure subsystem is responsible for designing and manufacturing the satellite structure while ensuring that the satellite can bear all the structural and thermal loads. It also maintains the temperature of all spacecraft components within certain limits.

Research Papers

Members of our club have been actively writing and publishing research papers in various conferences and platforms, not only in India but across the world. One such platform is the International Astronautical Congress(IAC).

Papers submitted in the tenure of STARDUST-2022

In 2022, 6 of our club members got the opportunity to present their papers in IAC, held in Paris.
They got the opportunity to not only showcase their ideas to the world but also meet various Indian and global space personalities.

Papers submitted in the tenure of STARDUST-2021

In 2021, 17 papers were submitted to IAC by our team members.
A few of them got the opportunity to travel to Dubai for their presentations.

Papers submitted in the tenure of STARDUST-2020

In 2020, IAC conducted a Cyberspace Edition of the conference due to COVID restrictions.
It was an entirely online event where 5 of our papers were presented.
