About our Team.

S.T.A.R.D.U.ST was founded in 2019 by a group of 3rd year UG students at Ramaiah Institute of Technology with a clear objective of designing and building a 2U Cubesat having a novel payload idea and launching it with the support and guidance of college Faculty.
